Computer Science & Engineering – Grade 6

In Grade Six, the boys focus laying the foundation needed throughout Middle and Upper School. Keyboarding will be emphasized in the first part of the semester and will be constantly developed throughout the year. The boys will be using code.org, a JavaScript-based platform to learn the basics of coding through building interactive games.

Penalty Kix

This is a two-player game. The kicker and the goalie. The kicker uses the keys a,w & d to kick the ball and the goalie will be using the arrow keys to block the ball. The first player to go over 100 points wins.

By Julian Brown & Sumair Shroff

Break

The objective of this game is to get the gold bars to earn points, while avoiding the robot in the process. Press m for background music, and use the arrow keys to move.

By Jack Dunn

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

Our Mission

We aim to inspire a boy's love for learning through computer science, engineering, and design. We see ICT (information and communication technology) as imagination machines, rather than replacements for what exists, these tools offer twenty-first century learners never before conceived mediums for self-expression.

Our lab serves as a multi-purpose space for study, debate, construction, tinkering, programming, and play. Creative thinking, thoughtful research, and cooperative investigation foster creative confidence and necessary skills to innovate and persevere.
